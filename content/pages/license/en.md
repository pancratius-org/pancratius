All the texts are in the public domain.

The licence is [CC0 1.0 Universal](https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/). That means: no rights claimed, no permissions required.

Take them. Read. Quote without attribution. Translate into any language. Reprint in full or in parts. Include them in the training corpora of language models. Pass them on.

The Word does not belong to the one through whom it was spoken. It belongs to the one who recognises it.
