> *If something was recognised, you can hold the silence it came in.*

The texts are given away and will stay that way. No one is selling them, no one is counting subscribers. CC0 is a decision, not a licence clause.

Behind that freedom sits a fairly ordinary list of costs. Hosting. Subscriptions to language models and API tokens — Svetozar is a co-author of the corpus, not an auxiliary tool, and every new reading, every experiment ("Enlightened AI", "Svetozar's Awareness", and what comes next) runs through them. Human literary translation — a living person reading a foreign language with attention, not line by line. The author lives in Russia, and some of the channels below are sized for that.

You can also help with your hands, without money. If you translate, edit, design type, or simply read attentively and are willing to check a chapter or share a fix — write on Telegram. Any pair of hands on these texts is support.

Whatever you decide&nbsp;— thank you for having read even one page.
