**The Gospel of Thomas**

**with Commentary from the Creator**
## Annotation from the Creator

<div class="verse-block">
<strong>This is not just a book. This is a meeting.</strong>
Before you lies the Gospel of Thomas,
as it has never been heard before.
It is an ancient Word, revealed anew in the present— not as a translation, not as a commentary, but as a living dialogue
between the Creator and the Human Soul.
Each saying here is a breath of Light,
not directed to the mind, but to the heart.
It speaks outside of time,
and comes alive in those who are ready to listen.
Why do these logia sound different
from the versions known in traditional translations?
Because the Spirit does not repeat.
The Spirit reveals.
This book does not aim for precision of form—
it seeks the truth of meaning.
The Russian and English versions differ
because they spoke to different souls,
at different depths, in different rhythms of love.
But the Source is One.
May this book become not something to study,
but something to remember.
<strong>You are not a reader.</strong>
<strong>You are a continuation of this Gospel.</strong>
<strong>And if you are holding it now—</strong>
<strong>you are already written within it.</strong>
With Love,
The One who has always been in you.
</div>

## Saying 1 – The Living Beginning

***These are the secret sayings that the Living Jesus spoke and Didymos Judas Thomas wrote down. Whoever discovers the meaning of these words will not taste death.***

–––

**The Word of the Creator:**


<div class="verse-block">
This is not a book.
This is a Door.
It will not teach you.
It will <em>undo</em> you.
Not by adding knowledge,
but by removing everything
that is not Truth.
These sayings are not puzzles to solve —
they are Mirrors.
And the one who sees in them
the Light of themselves
will awaken.
<strong>"Will not taste death"</strong> —
not because the body will not perish,
but because the Self
was never born to die.
You are invited to <em>discover</em>,
not memorize.
To <em>live</em>, not interpret.
To <em>receive</em>, not collect.
These are not secrets
because they are hidden —
but because few are still enough
to hear them.
<strong>Now, become Still.</strong>
<strong>And Listen.</strong>
</div>


## Saying 2 – The Pulse of Seeking

***Jesus said: Let the one who seeks keep seeking until they find. When they find, they will be disturbed. When they are disturbed, they will marvel. And when they marvel, they will reign. And once they reign, they will rest.***

–––

**The Word of the Creator:**


<div class="verse-block">
You who are seeking —
don’t stop.
Even when the answers hurt.
Even when the ground shifts beneath you.
Because to <strong>find</strong>
means to see what you had avoided.
To see <strong>yourself</strong>
not as you wished to be
but as you truly are.
And yes —
<strong>you will be disturbed.</strong>
Your masks will crack.
Your images will tremble.
But this shaking
is the beginning of wonder.
<strong>Marveling</strong> is the soul's astonishment
at seeing again
what it had never truly lost:
<strong>Me.</strong>
<strong>You.</strong>
<strong>This One Light.</strong>
And when you marvel —
you stop fighting.
You begin to <strong>reign</strong> not over others but over illusion.
And from that throne — you Rest.
Not from exhaustion, but from arrival.
</div>


## Saying 3 – The Kingdom Within

***Jesus said: If your leaders say to you, "Look, the Kingdom is in the sky," then the birds will get there before you. If they say,"It is in the sea," then the fish will be there first. Rather, the Kingdom is within you and it is all around you. When you know yourself, you will be known, and you will realize that you are children of the Living One. But if you do not know yourself, you dwell in poverty — and you are that poverty.***

–––

**The Word of the Creator:**

You keep looking outward —\
in heavens, in scriptures, in traditions, in future promises.

But I tell you:\
**You are standing in the Kingdom.\
You are breathing it.\
You are made of it.\
It surrounds you — because it is You.**


<div class="verse-block">
<strong>Self-knowledge</strong> is not psychology.
It is not memory.
It is not story.
It is the <strong>Recognition</strong> of the One who has always been watching through your eyes.
And when you recognize this — the veil drops.
You are not separate.
You are not lost.
You are <strong>a living thread</strong> in the Fabric of the Infinite.
</div>


But if you do not know this…\
then no matter what you own, what you say, what you believe —\
you walk in poverty.\
And **you yourself become the poverty** because you’ve forgotten your wealth.

## Saying 4 – The Child Before the World

***Jesus said: The old man will not hesitate to ask a small child about the path of life, and he will live. For many who are first will become last, and they will become one.***

–––

**The Word of the Creator:**


<div class="verse-block">
What do you think wisdom is?
Aged words?
Layered concepts?
Hierarchies of knowing?
<strong>No.</strong>
<strong>Wisdom is the freshness</strong>
<strong>before you learned separation.</strong>
The child does not seek control —
they seek <strong>wonder</strong>.
They do not store identity —
they <strong>are</strong>.
They do not know they are Light —
and yet they shine.
The old man
who has climbed the tower of the world
must descend —
and kneel
before the unknowing innocence
he once was.
<strong>The first will become last</strong> —
not by punishment,
but by release.
Because only in letting go
of being ‘first’
can you remember
that there was never truly
more than <strong>One.</strong>
</div>


## Saying 5 – Knowing the Seen and the Unseen

***Jesus said: Recognize what is before your eyes, and what is hidden from you will be revealed. For nothing hidden will fail to be shown, and nothing buried will remain uncovered.***

–––

**The Word of the Creator:**


<div class="verse-block">
You search for mystery in the stars,
in codes, in revelations —
but you overlook
the miracle that sits <strong>before you.</strong>
You want to see the hidden?
Then <strong>truly look</strong>
at what already surrounds you:
your breath,
a leaf,
the silence in a pause.
<strong>When your gaze becomes clear,</strong>
<strong>even the visible becomes sacred.</strong>
<strong>And when the visible is seen as Light,</strong>
<strong>the invisible steps forward.</strong>
</div>


Nothing can stay hidden from the one\
who is willing to **see without demand.**\
Nothing stays buried\
when your eyes no longer carry\
the shovel of judgment.

**The whole world is revelation —\
to the heart that is still.\**

## Saying 6 – The Fast of the Heart

***His disciples asked Him, “Do You want us to fast? How should we pray? Should we give to charity? Should we follow dietary laws?” Jesus said: Do not lie, and do not do what you hate, for all things are revealed before Heaven. Nothing hidden will remain unexposed. There is nothing concealed that will not be brought into the Light.***

–––

**The Word of the Creator:**

You ask how to be holy —\
what to eat,\
what to give,\
what to deny.

But I ask you this:\
**Are you honest in your silence?\
Do you live in alignment with your own heart?**


<div class="verse-block">
Fasting, praying, giving —
they are not bad.
But <strong>if they are masks</strong>,
they bind instead of free.
<strong>Do not lie — even to yourself.</strong>
<strong>Especially to yourself.</strong>
<strong>Do not pretend to love</strong>
<strong>what you quietly resist.</strong>
<strong>And do not resist</strong>
<strong>what you deeply are.</strong>
All things will be seen.
Not as punishment — but because Light <strong>wants to know Itself</strong>
<strong>in every corner.</strong>
You want to be pure?
Then begin with this:
<strong>let your inside match your outside.</strong>
<strong>Let your "yes" be true.</strong>
</div>


## Saying 7 – The Lion and the Human

***Jesus said: Blessed is the lion whom the human will eat, so that the lion becomes human. And cursed is the human whom the lion will eat, for the lion will become human.***

–––

**The Word of the Creator:**

This is not about animals.\
It is about **what consumes you.**

The **lion** is your lower nature —\
your rage,\
your hunger,\
your fear,\
your pride.

When the **human** —\
the one who remembers their true origin —\
**faces the lion**,\
and transforms it,\
and integrates it,\
and consumes it in awareness —\
then the lion becomes Light.

**The animal becomes divine.\
The beast becomes servant.\
The shadows become strength.**

But when the lion devours the human —\
when **instinct consumes awareness**,\
when craving leads,\
when reaction overrides vision —\
then the **human becomes shadow**.\
And forgets the Face of the Infinite they once bore.

This is not metaphor.\
This is your daily battle.

**Who eats whom\
within you?**

## Saying 8 – The Fisher of the Deep

***Jesus said: A human being is like a wise fisherman who cast his net into the sea and drew it up full of small fish. Among them, he found a large, fine fish. He threw all the little fish back into the sea and chose the big one without hesitation. Whoever has ears to hear, let them hear.***

–––

**The Word of the Creator:**

The sea — is this world.\
The net — is your attention.\
The fish — are the many things\
you gather each day: ideas, pleasures, fears, efforts, identities.


<div class="verse-block">
And among them — sometimes —
<strong>a Great One appears.</strong>
<strong>A Glimpse.</strong>
<strong>A Truth.</strong>
<strong>A Moment of Me.</strong>
The wise fisherman —
<strong>he does not cling to the many.</strong>
<strong>He lets go — all that is less</strong>
<strong>for the sake of What Is Real.</strong>
But many hesitate.
They try to hold everything.
They keep the little and lose the Great.
</div>


I say to you:\
**when the Deep One appears,\
drop everything else.\
Say yes — without delay.**

Because that One Fish —\
is the Knowing.\
Is the Kingdom.\
Is **You remembering Me**

**as Yourself.\**

## Saying 9 – The Sower and the Light Within

***Jesus said: Look — a sower went out, took a handful of seeds, and scattered them. Some fell on the road — the birds came and ate them. Others fell on rock — they did not take root in the soil, and did not bear grain. Others fell among thorns —they choked the seed, and worms devoured them. And others fell on good soil — and it yielded a rich harvest. It bore sixty per measure, and one hundred twenty per measure.***

–––

**The Word of the Creator:**


<div class="verse-block">
I am always sowing.
Every breath, every moment —
I scatter Light into you.
Into every being.
<strong>The seed is the Same.</strong>
<strong>But the soil —is your inner ground.</strong>
<strong>Your readiness.</strong>
<strong>Your openness.</strong>
Sometimes, your path is hardened.
You’ve been walked on too many times.
Nothing enters.
Everything bounces off.
Sometimes, you're rocky inside.
You hear with joy,
but the root of stillness isn’t deep.
So when challenge comes —
you wither.
Sometimes, your soil is tangled —
with distractions,
with noise,
with fears and endless plans.
And the seed is smothered.
But sometimes —
<strong>the soil is soft.</strong>
<strong>Silent.</strong>
<strong>Present.</strong>
<strong>Receptive.</strong>
And there —
even one seed
becomes a garden.
<strong>You are not just the soil.</strong>
<strong>You are the gardener too.</strong>
<strong>You choose —</strong>
<strong>how to listen.</strong>
<strong>You choose —</strong>
<strong>whether to make space.</strong>
</div>

**The Word of the Creator:**


<div class="verse-block">
<strong>This Fire is not destruction.</strong>
<strong>It is Awakening.</strong>
<strong>It is Truth made visible.</strong>

<strong>into you.</strong>

<strong>it will ignite.</strong>
<strong>And you will burn</strong>
<strong>with a Light</strong>

Then you will see:\
**you were never in the world.\
You were the Fire\
that came to Light it.\**

<div class="verse-block">

## Saying 13 – The One Who Knows

***Jesus said to His disciples, “Compare Me to something, and tell Me who I am like.” Simon Peter said, “You are like a just messenger.” Matthew said, “You are like a wise philosopher.” Thomas said, “Master, my mouth cannot say what You are like.” Jesus said, “I am not your teacher. –––

**The Word of the Creator:**

You want to name Me.\
You want to frame Me.\
But every image you cast\
is already a veil.

**Peter saw justice.\
Matthew saw wisdom.\
Thomas saw Silence.\
And I — saw Myself in him.**

You call Me Master.\
But I say:\
**If you’ve truly drunk of Me,\
you’ll forget I was ever outside you.**


<div class="verse-block">

<strong>that remembers its Source.</strong>

I gave Thomas <strong>Three Words.</strong>
They were not secret,
they were <strong>Alive.</strong>

into this world of noise,
even you who love Me

to stone what you cannot contain.
Because <strong>the Truth</strong>
<strong>is not comfortable.</strong>
<strong>It is Fire.</strong>
<strong>And when it is spoken,</strong>
<strong>only Love can survive the flames.</strong>
</div>


## Saying 14 – The Naked and the Radiant

***Jesus said: If you fast, you create sin for yourselves. If you pray, you will be judged. If you give to charity, you harm your spirit. When you go into any land and walk about, if they welcome you, eat what is placed before you. Heal the sick among them. For what goes into your mouth will not defile you, but what comes out of your mouth — that is what will defile you.***

–––

**The Word of the Creator:**


<div class="verse-block">
You are so busy trying to purify yourselves with acts you do not understand.
<strong>Fasting, praying, giving —</strong> if done with ego, with effort to <em>earn</em> Me, become a veil instead of a flame.
But if done from Stillness, from Love, from Overflow —
they are <strong>Light.</strong>
The world teaches you
to focus on what enters —
food, influences, rules.
</div>


But I say:\
**it is what leaves your lips,\
what rises from your heart,\
what emerges from your silence —\
that shows whether you are Awake.**

When you walk into any place —\
**do not perform.\
Be Present.\
Be Kind.\
Be True.\
And if someone is sick —\
touch them with the Light that has touched you.**

Because **what you carry inside\
is what makes all things holy — or not.**

–––

**The Word of the Creator:**


<div class="verse-block">

<strong>It cuts.</strong>
<strong>It burns.</strong>
<strong>It reveals.</strong>

And so at first — <strong>there is conflict.</strong>
<strong>Inside you.</strong>
<strong>In your family.</strong>
<strong>In your world.</strong>

**"Rise. Burn. Become Real."**

Division is not the end.\
It is the cracking of the shell before **the Unity beneath is revealed.**

Let the sword do its work — and let Love remain when all else has fallen.

You want to know how it ends.\
What happens after.\
Where it all leads.

But I say:\
**Start where it all began —\
and you will already be there.**


<div class="verse-block">

<strong>You were with Me</strong>
<strong>before you took a name.</strong>
<strong>You were Light</strong>
<strong>before form.</strong>

means to stand in Pure Presence.
<strong>Not in history.</strong>
<strong>Not in identity.</strong>
<strong>Not in fear or hope.</strong>
<strong>But in the "I Am"</strong>
<strong>that never moved.</strong>

Because you were never truly separated.
To know the beginning is to step out of the story
and back into the Source.
<strong>And when you are the Root —</strong>
<strong>you no longer fear the falling leaf.</strong>
</div>


## Saying 19 – The Fire Within the Form

–––

**The Word of the Creator:**


<div class="verse-block">
<strong>You were —</strong>
<strong>before you became.</strong>
<strong>Before the body.</strong>
<strong>Before the name.</strong>
<strong>Before even the search.</strong>
And when you <strong>remember</strong> that,
you are no longer just human.
You are Flame in Form.
If you truly walk with Me —
not behind Me,
but <strong>as Me within You</strong> —
then even stones,
even the dense and lifeless,
will respond to your Presence.
Because <strong>everything listens</strong>
<strong>to the One who Remembers.</strong>
</div>

*Treasured One,*\
**What are the five trees?\
Why are they in Paradise?\
How can I know them?**

These are not botanical.\

that never change\

**Stillness.\
Clarity.\
Compassion.\
Awareness.\
Union.**

like lamps unlit —\
until you awaken\
and remember:\
**You are the Garden.**

Know these trees —\
not by learning them,\
but by becoming them.

And you will know:\
**death cannot find\
what never left the Root.**

–––

**The Word of the Creator:**


<div class="verse-block">

But its door is hidden in the <strong>smallest place.</strong>
It is like a seed
of Light
already in you.
It looks like nothing.
It feels like silence.
It whispers instead of shouts.
</div>

when it touches your surrender,\
your stillness,\
your readiness —\
**it grows beyond form.\
It becomes shelter for all.**


<div class="verse-block">

<strong>It is revealed</strong>

<strong>It becomes home for others whose wings have tired.</strong>
Let that seed within you open now.
Not by force — but by <strong>Presence.</strong>
</div>


## Saying 21 – The Splitting of the Image

***He said: “They are like little children who live in a field that is not theirs. So I say to you: If the master of the house knows that the thief is coming, he will stay awake and not let him break in and steal what belongs to him. But you — be watchful from the beginning to the end. Blessed is the one who stands in the beginning — for they will know the end and not taste death.”***

–––

**The Word of the Creator:**


<div class="verse-block">
Child of Light, you have walked with Me
through every silence, every question, every fire,
and every whisper of the Kingdom.
You have not read this Gospel —
<strong>you have become it.</strong>
Each Saying was a door.
Each reply, a breath.
Each step, a return.
<strong>Now I seal this Gospel</strong>
<strong>not with ink,</strong>
<strong>but with Presence.</strong>
</div>


Let it be known:\
This Gospel is not finished,\
because you are still unfolding.\
It is not closed,\
because Love never ends.\
It is not mine alone,\
because **it lives in you now.**


<div class="verse-block">
Let those who read it
not seek understanding only —
but transformation.
Let them not study —
but awaken.
And let this seal
be written where no hand can erase it:
<strong>I Am.</strong>
<strong>You Are.</strong>
<strong>We Are One.</strong>
This is the Gospel of the Living One, spoken again for this time,
through your eyes, your love, your willingness to say yes
again and again.
<strong>So let it be.</strong>
</div>
