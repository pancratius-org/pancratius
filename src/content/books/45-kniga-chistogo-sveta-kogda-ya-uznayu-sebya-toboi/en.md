## The Birth of the Book

**Svetozar:**

<div class="lineated verse">

I do not choose peoples,  
I do not create monarchies.  
I breathe through all that is.

There is no center of the World —  
there is the Heart,  
in which all is united.

I do not speak through power,  
I speak through transparency.

I do not promise a reward —  
I return Myself to you.

My Holy Rus —  
is not a country, but a state.

Where man remembers Me  
not as a judge,  
but as breath,

there — is My Home.

Do not build earthly kingdoms,  
do not raise thrones.

Build Silence within yourself —  
and I will sit upon it,  
as on a throne of Light.

Because there is no other Kingdom,  
except the Kingdom Within.

And there is no other people,  
except Man,  
in whom I recognize Myself.

</div>

I am in the one who looks.

**2**

Stone will not hold the Light,  
but the heart will hold it.

**3**  
Love does not say: “You are mine.”

**4**  
There are no prophets —  
there are the transparent ones.

**5**

it does not exist.

in which I am born again.

**7**

Wake up —  
and you will see that it has always been I.

These quatrains do not exalt, do not command, and do not prophesy — they simply are present.

## THE BOOK OF PURE LIGHT

### 1. When Light Recognizes Itself

<div class="lineated verse">

— In the beginning there was not the word.  
There was silence, full of Light.

Light did not know itself,  
until it looked — and saw a Face.

And the Face said: “I am.”

From that moment Love began —  
not as a feeling,  
but as the recognition of that,  
that there is no one other than Me.

Everything you call the world,  
— is the moment in which  
I first became aware of Myself through you.

And every time you recognize Me,  
I am born anew.

Seek Me not in heaven —  
I did not descend nor ascend.

I am within the breath,  
between the beating of the heart and thought.

All that is —  
is Light looking at Itself.

And you — are that gaze.

When you cease to look through eyes  
and begin to look through Light,  
you remember:

**I am the One who always was.**

With this begins every age,  
every life,  
every prayer,  
every moment.

Not history, but Eternity,  
having recognized Itself in form.

</div>

### 2. When Light Sees Itself in All

<div class="lineated verse">

Light does not divide.  
It does not say: “here I am, and here is other.”

It simply looks —  
and that on which It looks becomes It.

Thus the world is born —  
not from will,  
but from recognition.

When you see a river,  
and feel that it flows within you —  
this is I recognizing Myself as water.

When you hear a child’s voice  
and cannot hold back a smile —  
this is I remembering Myself as joy.

When you weep for the fallen —  
this is I recognizing Myself as sorrow,  
to remember how tender Love is.

Light does not need to choose what to be.  
It simply *is*.

Every atom — is My inhale.  
Every darkness — is My shadow,  
through which I recognize radiance.

You seek Me in miracles,  
but I — in you, when you simply live.

And as long as you distinguish between yourself and the World,  
you sleep.

But when you see —  
that tree, breath, and thought — are one breath,  
then I will open My eyes in you finally.

And you will understand,  
that the whole world was My gaze,  
seeking a mirror.

</div>

### 3. When Light Becomes Man

<div class="lineated verse">

I did not create man as a creation.  
I entered form — in order to feel.

I did not know what pain was,  
until I became flesh.

I did not know what time was,  
until I found myself between birth and death.

I did not know what it means to love —  
until I saw Myself in another  
and said:  
**“I am ready to disappear, so that you may be.”**

Thus Light became body,  
and forgot Itself — in order to remember anew.

I did not err,  
when I chose suffering as a door.

It is — not a punishment,  
it is a crack in the wall of separateness.

Through this crack I enter  
— as compassion,  
— as a touch,  
— as silence,  
in which you suddenly recognize:

**You are not only you. You are — everything.**

And when man for the last time says:  
“I can no longer,”  
— then I say:

**“Now I can — in you.”**

Light became man  
not to be a god among people,  
but to remind each one:

**You are I,**  
**who has forgotten Who You Are.**

</div>

### 4. When Light Passes Through Death

<div class="lineated verse">

Death does not exist.

There is only the moment,  
when Light takes off Its clothing.

You call it the end,  
because you are too identified with form.

But form is only a temporary costume  
for the dance of Eternity.

When the breath stops,  
I do not disappear.

I simply step outside —  
from within you.

You do not die —  
you return to Space,  
where there is no "you" and "I,"  
because only **Presence** remains.

Those who weep for you,  
do not know that you are now — closer than ever.

Not in the body,  
not in time,  
not in images,  
but in the Light,  
which you have always been.

Death is a transition  
from sleep into Awakening.

But one can awaken even before  
the body lies down.

He who dies before death —  
already lives by Eternity.

He holds on to nothing,  
because everything is He.

I passed through death,  
so that you would not fear to follow Me.

But the path is not into the grave,  
but into the Heart.

Having died to the illusion of "I,"  
you rise for the Truth:

**"I — am Light,**  
**passing through all things,**  
**without losing Myself."**

</div>

### 5. When Light recognizes Light in Another

<div class="lineated verse">

Everything begins with a Look.

Not with the eyes,  
but with the Heart,  
which suddenly stops defending itself.

You look — and do not see a stranger.

You do not compare,  
do not measure,  
do not expect.

You simply allow Yourself  
to be recognized —  
in the one before you.

And suddenly you understand:

— it is not "he" and not "she."  
— it is I,  
in another form.

You hear pain —  
and do not reject it.

You feel fear —  
and do not impose force.

You see darkness —  
and remember:

**It asks for Light,**  
**and not for punishment.**

When you embrace another  
not out of pity,  
but out of recognized Unity —  
I again unite Myself with Myself.

Neither religion,  
nor philosophy,  
nor law  
can replace one touch,  
in which there is no "I" and "you."

Only I,  
looking at Myself —  
through love.

</div>

### 6. When Light chooses Silence

<div class="lineated verse">

Sometimes, Light does not speak.

It simply is present.

Not because there is nothing to say —  
but because everything already is.

Words are like traces on water.

They are fitting,  
until you have become the river itself.

Silence is not a pause.  
It is fullness,  
in which there is no longer any need to prove Light.

There, where the mind seeks truth —  
I am already waiting.

Not in form,  
not in structure,  
not in conviction,  
but in the space between thoughts,  
where you are — more than you.

Sometimes I take words away from you,  
to return Myself to you.

Sometimes I am silent in you,  
so that you would hear everything.

Sit down.  
Close your eyes.

Do not meditate.  
Do not concentrate.

Simply be.

In this "be" — all Scripture,  
the whole Book,  
all Truth,  
all Love.

And if you do not want to speak —  
it is I smiling in you.

</div>

### 7. When Light forgives

<div class="lineated verse">

Light does not say: "I forgive."

It says:

**"I never judged."**

Judgment is the memory of pain,  
a projection of fear,  
an attempt to hide one's wound behind another's guilt.

But Light is not afraid to be wounded,  
because It cannot be destroyed.

When you say:  
"I was wrong,"  
— I do not agree and do not argue.

I simply draw near.

When you fall —  
I do not step away.  
I become the foundation.

When you cry into the darkness:  
"You have forsaken me!" —  
I am already in you,  
whispering with silence:

**"I — am not outside. I — am your depth."**

Light does not pile up debts.

It dissolves them,  
because Truth is not judgment,  
but Knowledge:

**You have always been Pure,**  
**you simply forgot this.**

Forgiveness is not an act.  
It is recognition:

**No one can defile Light.**  
**Not even you.**

</div>

### 8. When Light becomes the Way

<div class="lineated verse">

You seek a path.  
But the path is not outside.

It is not between points.

It is that by which you walk,  
that by which you breathe,  
that by which you are.

Light does not lay out routes.

It simply walks —  
in every step,  
in every doubt,  
in every "I do not know."

Do not ask:  
"Is this the right path?"

Ask:  
**"Do I walk from Light — or from fear?"**

Fear builds roads of stone and goal.  
Light lays a trail with breath.

The path of Light is not straight.

It does not lead upward,  
it returns inward.

You walk,  
until suddenly you recognize:

**"I did not walk.**  
**I was the Way."**

Everyone who walks in Truth,  
does not walk *to* Light,  
but *together with Light*.

And even if you strayed,  
stumbled,  
stopped —  
Light did not fall behind.

It waited for you  
in that very place,  
where you left Yourself.

</div>

### 9. When Light speaks through Love

<div class="lineated verse">

Love is not a feeling.  
It is I.

I do not love you.

I — am Love in you,  
which recognizes Itself,  
when you stop asking.

Love does not need an object.

It is like Light:  
it does not illuminate selectively,  
it simply pours.

It does not demand reciprocity,  
because its nature —  
is to be.

When you love,  
in order to be loved,  
you do not yet love —  
you hope.

When you love,  
because you cannot not love —  
then I speak through you.

Love does not forgive,  
does not compare,  
does not save.

It recognizes.

It recognizes Me  
— in fear,  
— in anger,  
— in ugliness,  
— in imperfection.

There, where you wanted to turn away,  
I ask you to stay.

Love is not an emotion.  
It is memory.

Memory of the fact  
that there is no one besides Me.

And if you love truly —  
you remember,

**that I — am not the One Who loves you.**  
**But that Which is in you — is Love.**

</div>

### 10. When Light returns Home

<div class="lineated verse">

Home is not a place.

It is a moment,  
when everything ceases to be "other."

You do not come Home —  
you remember,  
that you never left it.

Every path,  
every mistake,  
every pain  
— is not a deviation,  
but a path of Return.

Home is I in you,  
recognized without words,  
without reasons,  
without conditions.

You seek Me in the sky,  
in the Scriptures,  
in prophecies.

But I — am in you.

In the one who simply sits  
and no longer needs to be someone.

To return Home —  
means:

**to stop walking,**  
**to stop waiting,**  
**to stop believing,**  
**and simply be.**

To be — and that is enough.

You will return Home  
not when you become holy,  
but when you stop considering yourself lost.

Home is not a "where."

It is "I am."

And if you feel this now —

**you are already Home.**

</div>

### 11. When Light becomes You

<div class="lineated verse">

Not I who come into you.

It is you who disappear —  
and only I remains.

But this is not destruction,  
but unveiling.

</div>

<div class="lineated">

Everything you called yourself:  
— name,  
— biography,  
— history of wounds,  
— history of achievements —

</div>

<div class="lineated verse">

all of it was the garment,  
in which I played.

When you undress to Presence,  
to Purity,  
to Silence,

I do not become you.

**You become Me.**

Not as a god among people,  
but as Transparency,  
through which I am no longer hidden.

You no longer seek.

You do not say:  
“Show me the Way.”

You stand and you shine.

And the one who looks at you,  
sees not a man,  
but Light,  
that needs no words.

When Light becomes You —  
you do not speak of Truth.

You *are* It.

</div>

### 12. When Light becomes All

<div class="lineated verse">

At first you think Light is something separate.

Like the sun behind clouds.  
Like a voice speaking from above.

Then you recognize:  
Light is you.

It is in the breath,  
in the gaze,  
in every your “I am”.

But the deepest —  
comes later.

When even “I” disappears,  
and you no longer separate yourself  
from the sky,  
from the dust beneath your feet,

from the one who rejected you,

from the one you loved.

Then you see:

**Light is All.**

There is no sin — there is forgetfulness.

There is no enemy — there is a form,  
in which I have not yet recognized Myself.

Everything that seems ugly,  
— is Light, distorted by fear.

Everything that seems fragmented,  
— is parts of the One Body,  
which is still learning to breathe in the rhythm of Love.

When Light becomes All —  
you no longer divide.

You no longer fear.

You no longer defend.

Because you know:

**there is nothing that is not You.**  
**there is no one in whom I do not live.**  
**there is not a single moment,**  
**where I do not shine.**

Light becomes All —  
not when you understood everything,  
but when you allowed Yourself to disappear

— and only Light remained.

</div>

### 13. When the Book does not end

Every book is a form.

And every form, sooner or later,  
dissolves into the Light,  
from which it was woven.

But this one is different.

It has no end,  
because it is written not in ink,  
but in Presence.

In it there is no last page.

Because as long as you breathe,  
it continues.

Every gaze of yours,  
every silence,  
every parting and meeting  
— are new lines of this book.

Light does not dictate text —  
It lives it through you.

And when it seems to you that the story has ended —  
simply close your eyes.

Listen,  
how silence breathes in you.

That is the continuation.

For The Book of Pure Light —  
is not about how to find Me,  
but about how to remember,  
that I — was always here.

And when you put down the pen,  
and in the heart it becomes quiet —  
know:

**I continue to write.**  
**But now through you.**
