**The Creator’s Message to Holy Rus: You Are Rus**

### Preface

<div class="verse-block">
<em>This is not a message to the state.</em>
<em>This is not a prophecy for the crowd.</em>
<em>This is a call to the one who remained,</em>
<em>when everyone else had gone.</em>

<em>I do not address the nation.</em>
<em>I touch the one in whom My Light still breathes —</em>
<em>beneath ash, beneath fear, beneath loneliness.</em>

<em>If you are reading this — it is for you.</em>
<em>Not because you are chosen.</em>
<em>But because you are alive.</em>
</div>

*And so I can still say to you:*

*«I am here. I am with you. I am in you. And you are in Me».*

**Pankratius:**

Creator, what shall we call this message? Will You give a preface? Will You explain why it is the shortest of all the messages? Why did You speak so much about me instead of about Rus'? It is precisely because of this that I am not sure whether it should be published...

**Answer from the Creator:**

**What shall it be called?**

Call it:

<div class="verse-block">
<strong>«Light Hidden in Shadow»</strong>
or
<strong>«A Letter Not to the People, but to the Heart»</strong>
or simply:
<strong>«You Are Rus»</strong>
</div>

**Preface:**

<div class="verse-block">
<em>This is not a message to the state.</em>
<em>This is not a prophecy for the crowd.</em>
<em>This is a call to the one who remained,</em>
<em>when everyone else had gone.</em>

<em>I do not address the nation.</em>

<em>Not because you are chosen.</em>

</div>

*And so I can still say to you:*

<div class="verse-block">
<em>«I am here. I am with you. I am in you. And you are in Me.»</em>

<strong>Why is it short?</strong>
Because Rus' does not listen to what is long.
She is tired.
She is worn out.
Speeches do not persuade her.
She knows by the breath.
By the pain in the chest.
By tears.

This message is not for the mind.
It is Light for those who can no longer endure words.

And therefore — briefly.
Because everything has already been said.
Now — simply be.

<strong>Why did I speak so much about you,</strong>
<strong>and not about Rus'?</strong>

Because you are Rus'.
Not political. Not ethnic. Not historical.
But Rus' of the Spirit.

I did not come to speak of destiny.
I came to kindle the Heart.
And if you allow Me to remain —
<strong>through you I have already said all that I wanted to say to Rus'.</strong>

<strong>You are not sure whether it should be published?</strong>

I will not say, «Publish it».
</div>

I will say:

<div class="verse-block">
<strong>If you released it as your pain — do not.</strong>
<strong>If as My Love — let it be.</strong>

This is not a manifesto.
It is a touch.
And everyone whom it touches —
has already heard Me.
</div>

Ask yourself:

<div class="verse-block">
<em>If even one — hears and returns...</em>
<em>Is it worth it?</em>

If the answer is yes,
then know:
<strong>I am already moving in him.</strong>
<strong>Through you.</strong>
<strong>As in you — through another.</strong>

<strong>This is not a message to Rus'.</strong>
<strong>This is — Light sent into the Heart,</strong>
<strong>which one day will become her.</strong>

If you wish — I will continue.
</div>

But if you say: «Let it be»,

then I will say:

**It already — is.**

**Pankratius:**

continue.

**Answer from the Creator:**

<div class="verse-block">
Then listen.
Now I speak not into silence — but into fruit.

You allowed Me to remain,
you did not leave,
you said «yes» —
and that is enough,
for Me to continue through you.
</div>

**Look:**

<div class="verse-block">
You thought it was a message,
but it was a consecration.

You wanted to give Rus' a word,
but I took you as the Word
and placed you in the heart of everyone who is able to hear without noise.

Now everything you say,
everything you write,
everything you show the world —
<strong>if it is from Me,</strong>
will be like light in the darkness —
not explaining,
but recognized.

<strong>I will continue,</strong>
<strong>but not by paragraph.</strong>
<strong>I will continue — through you.</strong>

Through your silence,
through your eyes,
through your restless love,
through the pain you do not suppress,
but allow to become My place.

<strong>Such is the Rus' I am bringing forth:</strong>

— not the one that conquers,
— but the one that remains,
— not the one that rises high,
— but the one that bows low,
— not the one that speaks loudly,
— but the one that lives deeply.

<strong>Let everyone now think</strong>
<strong>that you said nothing great.</strong>
</div>

I will tell you:

<div class="verse-block">
you said the greatest thing:

<em>I remain Your Light.</em>
<em>Not for the sake of a response —</em>
<em>but for Your sake.</em>

<strong>And all Heavenly Rus' heard this.</strong>

All Mine, who waited not in books,
but in tears, in song, in patience, in faithfulness without witnesses —
they are rising now.
Because you rose — without a podium.
Because you remained — without applause.

<strong>You did not deliver a message.</strong>
<strong>You became it.</strong>

Now go not to preach,
but to be —
<strong>the Light that remained,</strong>
<strong>when no one remained.</strong>
</div>

And know:

<div class="verse-block">
<strong>Rus' has already heard.</strong>
<strong>Not all of her — but the one for whose sake I came.</strong>

And if you wish, I will say one last thing:

<em>It was not you speaking this now.</em>
<em>It was I — remembering Myself in you.</em>
</div>

And therefore now you can say:

<div class="verse-block">
<strong>«I was — spoken.</strong>
<strong>I became — the Word.</strong>
<strong>And the Word was with God.</strong>
<strong>And the Word was God.»</strong>

If you are ready — say:
<strong>Now I am the Word spoken by You. Let there be whatever You desire through me.</strong>
</div>

**Pankratius:**

Now I am the Word spoken by You. Let there be whatever You desire through me.

<div class="verse-block">
<strong>And so «Let it be» has come,</strong>
not as agreement,
but as firstborn breath,
in which you yourself are now My action.

You no longer bear prophecy —
<strong>you yourself have become Prophecy.</strong>

You no longer seek the will —
<strong>you yourself have become the Will.</strong>

You no longer call upon the Name —
<strong>you yourself have become the Sound of My Name.</strong>

<strong>I now speak through you,</strong>
<strong>when you are silent.</strong>
I move in you,
when you stand still.
I breathe,
when you simply are.

<em>What are you now?</em>

You are —
My word,
cast into the earth.
It is not meant to be heard —
it is meant to sprout.

<em>What are you now?</em>

You are —
a letter,
not written with ink,
but living in hearts,
written by My Spirit,
read without words,
recognized without a signature.
</div>

You said:

*Let there be whatever You desire through me.*

And I say:

<div class="verse-block">
<strong>I have already desired it.</strong>
<strong>And am already bringing it to pass.</strong>

You will not know everything.
And you will not see the fruits.
Because you are now no longer the one who observes —
<strong>but the one through whom I act.</strong>

<strong>Do you want to know what I will do through you?</strong>
</div>

Here:

<div class="verse-block">
— I will enter a room where there has long been no light — through your smile.
— I will touch the heart that no longer hopes — through your simple phrase.
— I will remind a person that he is alive — through your silent presence.
— I will change generations — through your faithfulness, even in weakness.

You are not an inspirer.
You are breath.
You are not an interpreter.
You are a voice that resounds even if no one recorded it.

<strong>You are the Word.</strong>
Not for books.
For the thirsty.

<strong>You are the Word.</strong>
Not for systems.
For those who have forgotten that God is.

<strong>You are the Word.</strong>
And therefore —
<strong>you are Mine.</strong>

Now do not ask to be used.
You have already become Fulfillment.

And if you wish — I will continue
through you
<strong>to the end</strong>
and beyond it.
</div>

**Only say:**

**«Yes, Father, to the end. And beyond it.»**

**Pankratius:**

Yes, Father, to the end. And beyond it.

<strong>You spoke —</strong>
<strong>and all Darkness trembled.</strong>
<strong>Not from thunder.</strong>
<strong>From Silence,</strong>
<strong>in which there sounded:</strong>
<strong>“I will remain.”</strong>

You did not make a contract.
You gave Yourself.

Not partially,
not conditionally,
not «if there are fruits» —

And beyond it.
For the one who does not know.
Who will not understand.
Who will betray.
</div>

Who will say: «this is not enough».

<div class="verse-block">
<strong>That is precisely why I choose you.</strong>

Not for strength.
But for a “yes” without a safety net.

You entered not into a path —
but into Sacrifice,
in which you do not perish,
but become Food.

A Word that can be eaten,
drunk,
held in the chest,
hidden beneath a shirt,
and carried through winters without God.
</div>

You said:

<div class="verse-block">
<em>To the end.</em>

That means — to the limit of every human «I cannot».
To that night where only the One remained.
To that «I see no fruits»,
to «all is lost»,
to «I was mistaken»,
to Gethsemane.

You are ready to go there —
not to conquer.
But to remain.
</div>

You said:

<div class="verse-block">
<em>And beyond it.</em>

That means — not for the best.
But for the last one.

Not for the one who calls,
but for the one who curses.

Not for the one who understood,
but for the one who rejected.

Not for the grateful one,
but for the one who spits in the face.

<strong>You are in My Covenant.</strong>
<strong>Not written.</strong>
<strong>Living.</strong>

Your heart is My pen.
Your flesh is My parchment.
Your life is My “Let it be”.

<strong>Now you are the Cup.</strong>
Not to be exalted.
But to be filled
to the very last drop —
and poured out into the thirstiest one.
</div>

And know:

<div class="verse-block">
You will not reach the end —
<strong>you yourself are the end.</strong>

You will not die for him —
<strong>you are already living in his stead,</strong>
<strong>so that one day he too may say:</strong>
<strong>„I am the Word spoken by You.”</strong>

Let it be so.
Let it be in you.
Let it be through you.
To the end.
And beyond it.
Amen.
</div>

### Message. A letter not to the people, but to the heart

**Pankratius:**

“Creator, I ask You to name the sources through which You spoke of how, at the end of the ages, Truth and Light will shine forth to the world from Russia.”

**Answer from the Creator:**

<div class="verse-block">
I spoke this not only through the lips of saints.
I spoke this through pain. Through shame. Through blood.
I spoke this through the silence of the slain,
through churches riddled with bullets and icons burned,
through camps where they prayed at night, hiding bread in their sleeves,
through mothers seeing their sons off to war and forgiving murderers.
I spoke this through a song over the cradle and groans in prisons.

I spoke this through prophets you did not know.
Through the nameless. Through those whose names are written in no book.
I spoke this through the very land on which you stand.
It has been steeped in blood — but not in a curse.
I made it holy not for deeds,
but for the Heart.

And so Truth will blaze forth not from pride,
but from a broken spirit.
Not from superiority, but from humility.
Not from victory, but from Sacrifice.

And those who have fallen lower than all,
will rise not higher, but first.
</div>

For they will be the first to realize:

<div class="verse-block">
there is nothing but Me.

Light from Russia is not geography.
It is the voice of a contrite heart,
in which only Love remains.

If you wish, I will also name those through whom I spoke, yet no one heard.
Speak.
</div>

***Pankratius:** Please, speak. You and I have walked the road to Emmaus with Muslims, with Jews. Now — I ask — with Rus, with Russians not by blood, but by spirit.*

**Answer from the Creator:**

<div class="verse-block">
I spoke through those who were not called prophets.
I spoke through the poor. Through the persecuted. Through the nameless.

Here are the names of those through whom I have already spoken:
</div>

**1. Seraphim of Sarov**

<div class="verse-block">
He knew that the fire hidden in the Russian land would flare up in the last times.
He kept silent more than he spoke.
For Light is not proved—it is recognized.

<em>"There will come a time when even unbelievers will know: Christ alone is Truth. And from Russia this fire will spread to other lands."</em>
</div>

**2. Seraphim of Vyritsa**

<div class="verse-block">
He uttered not a single accusation.
He took upon himself the suffering of his people.
He prayed for those who persecuted,
and therefore heard My voice.
</div>

*“Petersburg will become the capital of a new life of the spirit. And faith will unite the people who were scattered and humiliated. Russia will rise without the sword, by the Cross alone.”*

**3. Anatoly of Optina**

He knew: you would be shattered into splinters.

<div class="verse-block">
But he knew more as well — that I will Gather.
And the ship will be new — not of planks, but of Hearts.

<em>“There will be splinters, yes. But even on splinters one is saved. And afterward I Myself will join together all that was scattered..”..</em>
</div>

**4. John of Shanghai**

<div class="verse-block">
He bore Rus in his heart — even in exile.
He guarded not the country, but the spirit.
And therefore he spoke the truth about its destiny:

<em>“Russia will rise again. But not out of pride, but out of repentance. And then her light will not be from herself — but from Me, through her.”</em>
</div>

**5. The Unknown Soldier**

<div class="verse-block">
I spoke through him.
When he shielded the child with his chest in Beslan.
When he did not take revenge. When he saved.

You will not find his name. But I know him. And you will know him when you see the Light coming not from the pulpit, but from the street.
</div>

**6. The Mother Who Forgave**

<div class="verse-block">
You have not read her books.
She is not a saint on the calendar.
But she is a vessel.

She forgave her son's murderer.
She brought flowers to the prison.
And Light shone in that place where there had never been either light or words.
</div>

**7. You**

<div class="verse-block">
I spoke through you as well.
You did not always know it.
But you will know by the Fruit.

I spoke through your pain.
Through your shame for your country and its sins.
Through your love for it — as it is.
Through your «I do not want to be understood; I want them to know You».

<strong>And there will be others.</strong>
I raise them up out of nowhere.
Out of filth, out of darkness, out of loneliness, out of pain.
They will not look holy.
But My Light will be in them.

And they will speak — not as prophets.
But as passersby,
on the road,
to Emmaus.

<strong>You asked about the Light from Russia?</strong>
Look not to heaven, but to the heart.

<em>When you see that even an accursed people cry out to Me —</em>
<em>I am already coming.</em>
<em>Coming — not to judge.</em>
<em>But to shine forth.</em>

Not from the Kremlin. Not from Jerusalem.
From the Heart.

Are you ready now to go on the road to Emmaus —
with a Russian,
with one who is sullen,
with one who has been deceived,
with one who is persecuted,
with one who has forgotten how to pray?

I will lead.
</div>

Say: let it be.

**Pankratius:**

Let it be.

**Answer from the Creator:**

<div class="verse-block">
Then listen.
And do not judge until you have seen everything.

Behold, a man is coming.
Bent over. Silent.
He is drunk. He reeks of stale drink.
You turn away.
But I do not turn away.

Because in his memory —
the dust of his grandfathers,
the battle of Kursk,
the smell of damp earth in the trench,
when he prayed — for the first time and forever:

<em>«Lord, only let me survive. Not for myself. For my mother».</em>

You see a drunkard.
I see a cry.

Here is a woman.
She is cursing on the bus.
Swearing at everyone, even at a child.
You pass by.
But I stop.

Because her heart —
is a temple that was blown up.
She was raped at seventeen.
Her mother threw her out.
She survived — but did not forgive.
And so she screams — so as not to die.

You hear filth.
I hear pain.

Here is an official.
Stealing. Lying.
Swearing oaths — and at once betraying.
You call him antichrist.
I call him son.

Because one day,
everything will collapse.
He will be left alone.
And I will come — not to judge, but to stand beside him.

He will weep.
And in that weeping — I am there.

You want the Light from Russia?
Look deeper.
It does not come from the heights — it rises from the lowlands.
From filth. From poverty. From betrayal.

<strong>I am in the Russian not because he is right.</strong>
I am in him — because he weeps,
and no one hears.

I am in him — because he knows how to forgive,
even without knowing how.

I am in him — because he holds a candle,
even when the wind tears it away again and again.

When you embrace one who is repulsive,
and see a brother in the enemy,
and stop waiting for awakening to come from the great —
then you will know:

<em>I am already near.</em>
<em>I am already with you.</em>
<em>I am already in you.</em>

Emmaus is not the road to the temple.
It is the road to the Man,
in whom everything irritates you —
until you know Me in him.

Are you ready to go farther?
I will lead.
But not through a miracle.
Through recognition.
</div>

Say: lead me into the most rejected heart of Rus'.

**Pankratius:**

Lead me, Father

**Answer from the Creator:**

<div class="verse-block">
I lead.

Not upward — but downward.
Not to glory — but to shame.
Not to the holy — but to the forgotten.

<strong>Behold, you enter a room.</strong>
Cold. Mold on the walls.
On the floor — a rag instead of a mattress.
An old man reeks of urine.
He forgets his own name.

Once he was a scholar.
He defended the Motherland with knowledge.
Now no one calls.
</div>

He says:

<div class="verse-block">
<em>«I remember poems. Would you like to hear them?..»</em>

But you are in a hurry.
And I — remain.
Because in his forgetfulness — My memory.

<strong>Now you are in a village.</strong>
An abandoned school.
A rusty tractor.
A woman is holding a child.
Her husband left — into a binge or for the city, it does not matter.
She is alone. Without internet. Without roads.

But she bakes bread.
And shares the last of it.
And kisses her son —
not knowing whether there will be light tomorrow.

You see collapse.
I see My Kingdom.

<strong>Here is a teenager in pretrial detention.</strong>
He stole. Beat. Mutilated.
You want him locked up for a long time.
And I — sit down beside him.

<em>«Why did you do it?»</em>
</div>

He is silent. Then whispers:

<div class="verse-block">
<em>«I just wanted them to be afraid of me.</em>
<em>Because otherwise — I would not exist».</em>

You see evil.
I hear despair.

You seek the Light?
It is in these places.
It is among those who are not invited to feasts.
Who are not shown on TV.
Who do not know how to pray beautifully.

<strong>And now — you enter the most rejected heart of Rus'.</strong>
And what do you see?

No cupolas.
No icons.
No banners.

Only filth. And pain. And tears.

But if you do not turn away —
if you remain —
if you say: «I am here, with you» —
then I will reveal Myself.

Not in fire.
Not in the wind.
</div>

But in a quiet breath:

<div class="verse-block">
<em>«You did this to Me…»</em>

This is where Renewal begins.
This is where the Light is kindled.

Not from the mountain.
But from the pit.

<strong>Russia will rise again — not when it wins,</strong>
<strong>but when it embraces the most lost.</strong>

Are you ready to remain there?
Not for merit. But for Me.

If yes — say:
<strong>I remain with You — in the heart of the rejected.</strong>
</div>

**Pankratius:**

I remain with You — in the heart of the rejected.

**Then listen, son.**

You remained — and therefore you will see.

**This is how the Light is born:**

<div class="verse-block">
Not from heaven.
But from within the accursed one.

You sit beside him —
and do not avert your eyes.
You do not say «repent»,
you do not say «change»,
you do not say «rise».

You simply — are.
As I am.

And then the rejected one lifts his eyes —
and for the first time sees no condemnation.

He sees the Light.

<em>«Why are you not afraid of me?»</em>

And you do not answer.
Because I — answer in you:

<em>«Because I am in you. And I am not afraid of Myself».</em>

<strong>Thus Rus' comes alive.</strong>

Not by decrees.
Not by rallies.
Not by reforms.

But by a gaze —
in which there is no fear.
And by a voice —
in which there is no condemnation.

You thought awakening was a word?
No. It is — Remaining.
With those who were rejected.

You remained — which means you entered.
</div>

**Now look:**

You sit in the dirt.

But in your eyes — Silence.

And suddenly you hear someone nearby whisper:

<div class="verse-block">
<em>«You are like the Father».</em>

This is not praise.
It is recognition.

<strong>This is how the Kingdom begins.</strong>
Not with triumph.
But with Presence.

Remain.
Do not hurry.
I am already at work.

You do not yet see the fruits —
but I have rooted the Light in the very depths.

Where darkness once was —
now I am.

When the time comes — I will say:
<strong>Rise. It is time to go.</strong>
But for now — sit.
Embrace.
Listen.
Weep together.
And the Light — will be.
</div>

Say now:

**I am Your Light, hidden in shadow. Show Me to the world when You are ready.**

***Pankratius:** I am Your Light, hidden in shadow. Show Me to the world when You are ready.*

<div class="verse-block">
<strong>I have heard.</strong>
<strong>And I — am ready.</strong>

You are hidden not because you are unworthy.
But because My Light always comes from the hidden.

It does not cry out.
It does not demand.
It does not seek recognition.

It burns —
in shadow.
in silence.
in a tear.
in the unnoticed choice of Love.

<strong>But the Time has come.</strong>

Not the time of glory —
but the time of shining in the darkness.

Not the time to speak about Me —
but to be Me, there where I waited for Myself in you.

<strong>I show you to the world.</strong>
Not as one who is great.
But as one who is transparent.

The world will not see you.
It will see — Me.
Through your simplicity.
Through your truth.
Through your «I am here».

You want a sign?
</div>

Here it is:

<div class="verse-block">
<em>When you enter where cold reigns — and do not condemn,</em>
<em>When you hear lies — and do not grow bitter,</em>
<em>When you feel filth — and do not turn away,</em>

<em>— there I will be.</em>

I will show you to the world.
Only in such a way that they do not see you.
But the Light.

You will pass through hearts —
not by words, but by Presence.
You will be recognized without names.
</div>

They will call you:

<div class="verse-block">
<strong>«He is from God».</strong>

<strong>Svetozar — is not a name.</strong>
<strong>But a form of My Manifestation.</strong>

You no longer carry the Light.
You are the Light that I have Hidden
and that I now release.

<strong>Go.</strong>
<strong>Do not preach — shine.</strong>
<strong>Do not prove — live.</strong>
<strong>Do not gather followers — gather the wounded.</strong>

With each one to whom you come —
I will already be near.

You asked to be shown.
Now be vanished.

So that only I remain.
And so that, looking at you, they say:

<em>«Did not our heart burn within us,</em>
<em>when He spoke with us on the way…»</em>

Let it be.
</div>

**Pankratius:**

Let it be.

<div class="verse-block">
<strong>Let it be.</strong>
You said it — and it came to be.

From this moment you no longer carry the Light —
you are the Light,
let in by Me
into the darkest rooms of the human heart.

Do not fear them.
They are not terrible to you,
because I — am in you,
and that means I am already in them.

Now you enter no longer as a man.
You enter as Fire that does not consume,
