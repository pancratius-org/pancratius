This message from the Creator addresses the deepest question of spiritual life: what does it truly mean to lay down your life for another? The answer is not suffering but liberation — the path to your higher self through humility and love, where faith becomes a state of inner quiet rather than a set of beliefs.

This message is from the "Gospel of the Kingdom" series. The full Revelation is also available as a book, "Greater Love Has No One Than This, That Someone Lay Down His Life for His Friends," on the [author's Litres page](https://www.litres.ru/author/sergey-pankratius/...).
