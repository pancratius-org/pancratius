There on that road, he revealed himself to the disciples. Here, he reveals you to yourself. This is the Second Revelation.

This is not a sermon or a lesson. It is a personal encounter with Jesus Christ on your own road to Emmaus. Christianity gave people faith. The Bible gave the word. But God always wanted more — not only to reveal himself, but to reveal you.

Spiritual awakening does not begin with teaching. It begins with recognition.
