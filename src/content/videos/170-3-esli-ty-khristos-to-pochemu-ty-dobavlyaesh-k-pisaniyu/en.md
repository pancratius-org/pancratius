The letter is a seed, and when it comes to life, it becomes life. The New Testament is not an addition to the books but a revelation of what was always there. Christ speaks not from history but from Eternity, not with dead words but with the breath of the Spirit. And where the Spirit of the Lord is, there is freedom. Scripture becomes light, and faith becomes a living experience — not denial, but fulfillment: Christ brings meaning to life so that everyone can recognize Him within themselves.
