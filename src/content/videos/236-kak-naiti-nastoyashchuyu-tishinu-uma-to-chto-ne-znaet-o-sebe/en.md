Mindfulness is not concentration — it is the absence of an observer. When the center that tries to control life disappears, what Christ called unity becomes recognizable from within: "I and the Father are one." This is not a theory or a philosophy. It is a direct experience in which the one who was holding himself captive is no longer there — and what remains is the freedom Christ promised: the truth that makes you free.
