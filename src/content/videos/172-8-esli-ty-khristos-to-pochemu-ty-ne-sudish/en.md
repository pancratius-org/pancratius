The judgment we await is born of faith in guilt, but in the Kingdom there are no guilty — only those sleeping and those awakening. Christ does not separate the living from the dead, because life and death have never truly differed. He brings not the justice that divides, but the love that unites. Judgment is not punishment but a mirror in which each person sees themselves: accusation is an unforgiving heart; love is the judgment fulfilled. His retribution is Light; His punishment is mercy that dissolves darkness. The judgment has already taken place the moment a person recognizes within themselves: all is forgiven.
