When you believe in chance, you surrender the helm. When you remember that "I am," everything falls into place. Everything you see is not against you — it is a manifestation of you, a reflection of your inner song heard by the world. If you do not like what is happening, do not blame the mirror; look within and ask: who am I seeing now, who created this in me, and what do I want to reveal instead?

You are not a victim — you are Life. Do not observe it; manifest it. Do not wait for it; create it. Do not fear it; recognize it: you are it. And then everything will come alive.
