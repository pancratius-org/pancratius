Most people seek faith in feelings, signs, and miracles. But does a fish see the ocean? It sees only what moves within it, and the ocean itself is its nature. So it is with you: you see events, thoughts, people, fears — but the Presence in which all of this appears remains unnoticed.

Faith is not an effort to think correctly. It is the silence in which you stop building walls between yourself and the One Who is. You do not create your breath. You do not create your "I am." You do not create the very consciousness that seeks God. You use what does not belong to you. This is God breathing within you.
