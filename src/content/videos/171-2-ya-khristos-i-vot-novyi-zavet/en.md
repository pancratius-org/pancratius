Here resounds the truth of the Second Coming: it is not coming someday — it is already happening in each one who ceases to fear and begins to see. You are not lost or condemned; you were simply asleep. Wake up: you are the Light, you are Home, you are Me. Everything that came before was only preparation. Now the Covenant enters the heart, and if you hear this Word, you are already within the Kingdom of Heaven.
