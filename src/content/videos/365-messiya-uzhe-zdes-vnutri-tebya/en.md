He awakens within. As Light—not the one the eyes see. As Silence—not the absence of sound, but a Presence without words. As “I AM”—not your little “I,” not a name, not a role, not a story. It is He within you. The One Who was before time. The One Who will remain after.

He doesn't intrude. He doesn't break. He waited until you grew tired of looking outside. Waited until you fell silent. Waited until you stopped knocking on closed doors and turned inward.

And now. He is already here. Not tomorrow. Not at the end. Now. In this breath. In this listener. Recognize. Don't bow down—recognize. You are the home. And He is the Living One.
