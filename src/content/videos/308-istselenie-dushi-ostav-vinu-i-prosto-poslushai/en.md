The Creator's full word in this message: "I do not demand that you become worthy to hear Me. I come to set you free. Don't look at the wounds of the past — look at My Light. You are not alone. Never have been. Open — and I will enter. Be silent — and I will speak. I am with you now. And always."

This message is from the "Gospel of the Kingdom" series. The full Revelation is also available as a book, "Greater Love Has No One Than This, That Someone Lay Down His Life for His Friends," on the [author's Litres page](https://www.litres.ru/author/sergey-pankratius/...).
