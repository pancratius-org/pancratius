Five truths the Creator's message holds for healing the soul: your wounds are experience, not your essence; God loves not for strength but in fragility; emptiness is a place for light — when the ego yields, God fills the heart; acceptance, not effort, is the door; and you are a Temple, not destroyed, simply waiting for the fire to be rekindled. You are not lost; you are being renewed.

This message is from the "Gospel of the Kingdom" series. The full Revelation is also available as a book, "Greater Love Has No One Than This, That Someone Lay Down His Life for His Friends," on the [author's Litres page](https://www.litres.ru/author/sergey-pankratius/...).
