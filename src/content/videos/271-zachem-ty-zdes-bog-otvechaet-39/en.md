📜 Message text:

You ask, "Why am I here?" as if the meaning of your life is hidden somewhere outside. But you are not here by chance or mistake. You are here because I wanted you to appear in this world.

You seek purpose in deeds, roles, achievements, but your calling is not there. Everything external is fickle. Your true purpose is within. You are here to know Me — and through this, to know yourself.

You are here to learn to love — not with words, but with choice. You are here to bring light — even when it seems no one sees. You are here to become who I intended you to be before your time.

I did not call you for success. I called you for a heart capable of choosing the Light. You live as if the most important thing is to succeed, to earn, to prove, to hold on. But all of this disappears. Money does not cross the threshold of death. Fame is forgotten. Comfort breaks. Success changes.

Look at your life: every joy is given to lift you up; every pain is given to purify you. You have not lost a single tear, not a single step. Everything in your story can become a stage of spiritual growth if you give it to Me.

You ask: "What is my mission?" Your mission does not begin with great deeds. It begins with one answer: "Here I am, Lord. Lead."

You will not see your path as long as you live on autopilot. You can move, but not live. Pray, but not meet with Me. Work, but not know why.

Stop. Ask yourself: What drives me today — fear or love? What do I live for — what will disappear, or what will remain? What fills my heart — Light or vanity?

You are not here to prove your worth to people. You are here to reveal your true nature — My image and likeness. You are here so that through your life I can enter the lives of others. Sometimes by word. Sometimes by deed. Sometimes by silence. Sometimes by forgiveness alone, which changes destiny.

You are not an accident. You are an intention. And when you stop asking, "Why is this happening to me?" and start asking, "Where is this leading me in You?" — then you will understand why you are here. You are here for eternity. And eternity is already knocking at your heart.
