How does the "I" dissolve? What is true resurrection — not the death of the body, but the death of the ego? How do you read Scripture as a map of consciousness? How do you see God — not outside, not within, but as the very substance of being?

What people call "the end times" and the Apocalypse is the end of the era of the divided self — the end of divided perception, and the beginning of a new world. The Book of Silence is the message of Jesus Christ for those who will live after the divided self.

Spiritual awakening is not a religion. It is a transformation of consciousness. The revelation of God is not outside. God is within — as the foundation of existence itself. This is the territory of Light. Here, the voice of the Creator resounds.
