God is telling you today: stop controlling everything.
This saving isn't love. It's fear.

You feel responsible for everyone. For your loved ones. For those who are suffering. For those who are lost.
You think: If I loosen my grip, everything will collapse.
And you carry it. And you grow tired. And inside you quietly die.

This is the weariness of the soul, not the body. You are trying to replace God with yourself.
But the world is supported by Him. And you are supported by Him.
And you don't need to hold on to anything else.

It's time to let go of the burden. The world didn't ask you to carry it.
The world asked you to be. To be alive. To be with an open heart—like in a house where the door is open.

Christ carried the Cross. But He didn't carry people as a burden.
He carried them in His heart as love. This is precisely what spiritual restoration is: to stop dragging and start containing.

If you're wondering, “How can I stop carrying everything?”—the answer is here.
It's not a technique. It's a role shift.
From savior to receiver.

Overcontrol destroys. Control is an illusion of security.
The only way to let go of control is to trust in the One who holds everything.
And then you will see: carrying the world doesn't mean carrying it on your shoulders.
It means giving it a place in your heart.

Stop being a savior. Simply become someone who is open.
This is your true strength. Not the one that carries. But the one that contains.

I bless you with lightness.
The world will not collapse. And you will breathe a sigh of relief.
