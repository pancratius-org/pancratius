Our childhood wounds, with an invisible hand, dictate our choice of partner and draw us into cycles of pain. Christ speaks here and now: true intimacy is not merely the touching of bodies but a profound merging of souls. Many couples live for years under the same roof without ever truly meeting, hiding behind social masks and the ghosts of the past.

The deep psychology of relationships is inseparable from the spiritual path. Until we experience healing through a personal encounter with the Creator, we will endlessly project our inner pain onto those we love. True spiritual relationship begins where selfish pretension ends and total acceptance is born — the great journey from the childish "I want" to the divine "we are."
