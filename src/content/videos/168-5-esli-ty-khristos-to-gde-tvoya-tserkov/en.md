Anyone who recognizes Christ in themselves becomes this stone. The Church is not an organization but a breath, not a ritual but a state of unity. Where two or three are gathered in His Name, there is the Church. She lives by mercy; her clergy are Love, her priest is the Heart, her Patriarch is the I Am. The Church of Christ is not in form, but in the living Light that is revealed in each one.
