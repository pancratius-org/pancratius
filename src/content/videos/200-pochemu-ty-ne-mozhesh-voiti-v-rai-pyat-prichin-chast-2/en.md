In the parable of Lazarus, the rich man cried out: "Send word to my five brothers!" Those five brothers are not five people — they are the five senses that have not yet recognized the Truth. They are not sinful; they are blinded. They were given to you to become guides of the Light. But you gave them power, and they became blind guides.

This is not a religion. It is remembering who you are beyond the harness.
