The Kingdom of Heaven is not a place we go to after death — it is a state of consciousness. The great mistake of religious searching is to look outward for what can only be found within. Spiritual awakening is not an achievement to pursue; it is the moment you stop waiting and simply begin to be.

This message is for those who are tired of rituals and hunger for a living presence. God is within. Recognize it now.
