The Cross is an intersection: the vertical of the Spirit and the horizontal of man, and in their meeting, Christ is born. Calvary is within you, in the moment when you choose love over fear. This is the crucifixion of the mind and the resurrection of the heart. Christ no longer dies; He lives in everyone. And when you love and do not judge, this is your cross — now luminous, without pain, only radiant Light.
