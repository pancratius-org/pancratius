The life you are tired of is a mind caught between past and future. But real life is right here — in this breath, in the simple "I am." There is no darkness there, only presence and light. This is where the Kingdom of God, of which Christ spoke, opens. This is where God touches you and says: you are Mine. You are life itself.

This word holds a way out of despair. Not a promise of an easy road, but a real awakening: the chance to truly live for the first time, without fear, without a mask, without fatigue. Even in the deepest darkness, there is always light. And that light is you.
