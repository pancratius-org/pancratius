His word always arrives in silence and brings peace, even when it reveals the truth. If you feel heavier after a thought, it is not Him. If you feel lighter, it is always Him. The Creator speaks in a way the heart recognizes without proof: in peace, in gentleness, in a simple inner "yes."

To hear Him, you don't need to shout or earn it. You need only be still. Release tension. Open up. He is closer than any thought, and He responds to those who can pause — even for a moment.
