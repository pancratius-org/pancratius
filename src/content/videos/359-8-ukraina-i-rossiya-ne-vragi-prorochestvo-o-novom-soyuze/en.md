The spiritual awakening of nations. Ukraine and Russia, Belarus—the homeland of a single soul.
Orthodoxy, the Bible, Jesus Christ—the prophecies of a New Union are coming true.
Are you used to thinking: Holy Rus' is about empire, about borders, about Orthodoxy-autocracy-nationality? No. It is a space where God is recognized in one's neighbor. Where a passport doesn't matter, only one thing matters—you are not an enemy, you are a brother.
Kyiv is the mother of Russian cities: neither Ukrainian nor Russian—a common source.
Moscow is the gatherer of lands: not an imperial center, but a place where they understood: “We may perish separately, but we will survive together.”
Minsk is the quiet keeper of memory, where they know: brotherhood is not abolished by wars.
These are not three countries. These are three voices of one soul.
The prophecy of a New Union is not politics. This is a spiritual unity that Russian history has failed to destroy with any war.
Ukraine is not anti-Russia. Ukraine is Rus', which was long convinced that brotherhood is oppression. The disease passes. And when it passes, the Ukrainian will not become Russian. He will remain Ukrainian. But he will remember. The Bible already predicted: the healing of the gaze precedes the healing of the land.
Russia must cease to be the center. It must become the heart. The heart does not command—the heart warms. The brotherhood of nations is not built by decrees. The philosophy of life long ago provided the answer: the unity of nations is born where people cease to see their neighbors as enemies.
Orthodox songs, prayer, the New Testament, mindfulness—all of this called for one thing: spiritual development is impossible without peace. Spiritual enlightenment is the healing of the gaze. Self-awareness means seeing in Ukrainians, Belarusians, Kazakhs, and Georgians not a threat, but a reflection. Peace and love are not weakness. This is Holy Rus. Self-development begins with one question: do I see a brother—or an enemy? The meaning of life has already been answered. Awakening has already begun. Faith in a new covenant of relations between nations is not a utopia. It is a spiritual constitution. Peace in Ukraine is not a political project. It is healing.

Brothers forever is not a slogan. It is an enlightenment of consciousness, an awareness of oneself as part of a single whole.
