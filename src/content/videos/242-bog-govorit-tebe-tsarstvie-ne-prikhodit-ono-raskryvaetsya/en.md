When you stop looking outward and for the first time see the depth that gazes back, you enter the Kingdom without taking a single step. The Kingdom is not "there" or "later." It is here, when the one who seeks it outside themselves disappears.

This is the Gospel of the Kingdom: I am here. You are in Me. And nothing has ever separated us except the gaze that looked outward.
