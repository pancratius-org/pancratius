This book does not ask how to fix you — it asks who, exactly, is suffering, afraid, clinging, and growing weary inside. Silence here is not emptiness, and not a withdrawal from the world. Silence is the place where, for the first time, the inner noise stops screaming.

If anxiety, burnout, the search for meaning, or spiritual awakening has brought you here — perhaps this book will prove not a theory, but a door. Not to another life, but to the space where life, for the first time, ceases to be a struggle.
