This is the twentieth letter of the Gospel of the Kingdom, where the Creator Himself says: "I do not come in parts. I enter — whole. But only if you are willing to reveal everything." God is not the enemy of your secrets. He has come to restore you to Himself. Don't be afraid to be seen. Right there, where you have been hiding, He calls you to be free.
