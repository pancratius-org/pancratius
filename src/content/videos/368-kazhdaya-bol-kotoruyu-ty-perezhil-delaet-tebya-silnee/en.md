You think you're starting from scratch. But that's not true.
Every day you open your eyes—not empty. But saturated.

If you're tired, if it seems like your strength has drained away—stop and look at your hands.
They remember everything you've endured. Every fear you've gone through. Every loss you've survived.
Every morning you didn't feel like it, but you got up.

It's not a burden. It's a foundation.

God doesn't give you a blank slate—He gives you a slate already written: “I made it.”
And this “I made it yesterday” becomes your ticket to today's “I can.”

You're not a newbie. You're a veteran. And veterans know how to win.
