People often fear freedom more than submission. It is far easier to live in a system where God is distant and unattainable than to accept that the source of life and light can be found within oneself. History shows that this living word was gradually transformed into formula, dogma, and grounds for accusation. What could have been a direct experience became part of a religious institution.

This is not about the destruction of faith, but about understanding why humanity sometimes turns away from the light — even when it sounds right before them. Is it possible today to hear again the words that once changed the world?
