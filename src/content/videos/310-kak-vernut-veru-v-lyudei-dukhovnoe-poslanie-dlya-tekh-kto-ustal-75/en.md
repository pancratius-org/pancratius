This message from the Creator is for those tired of being disappointed in themselves and others. It explores how to forgive betrayal — not only of others but of yourself — and how to remain open to the world when trust has been broken. The Gospel of the Kingdom is the news that God already lives within you, and that every person, even one who has hurt you, carries a hidden spark of the same light.

This message is from the "Gospel of the Kingdom" series. The full Revelation is also available as a book, "Greater Love Has No One Than This, That Someone Lay Down His Life for His Friends," on the [author's Litres page](https://www.litres.ru/author/sergey-pankratius/...).
