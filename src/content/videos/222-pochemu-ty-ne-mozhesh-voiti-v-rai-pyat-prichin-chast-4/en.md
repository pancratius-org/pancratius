The seventh is different. He is not a brother — he is you. He does not judge, does not feel, does not react. He simply is. He is the witness, the Self within you. You have always known him: in childhood, in silence, in beauty, in horror. You simply forgot.

Five levels are the senses. The sixth is the mind. The seventh is the Light. When you recognize the Seventh, the Sixth will fall silent, and the Five will bow. Only then will you be able to enter the Feast.
