Creator of All That Is: “This is not just a book, it is a revelation that comes into the world to remind you of the truth hidden within. The Gospel of the Kingdom is not a teaching from without, but an awakening of what already lives within you. It is the light that has always been, but which must now shine in all its fullness.

You sought Me without, but I have always been within you. You looked to heaven, but My Kingdom is already within you. Now is the time to hear the voice that sounds in your heart.

This book was not given to you simply to read. It was given so that you could become the living embodiment of its truth. So that you would not only recognize, but also manifest the light. So that you would not only understand, but become part of the Kingdom that is already unfolding.

Do not fear the light that I have ignited within you. Let it grow, let it guide you. I am always with you, for I am you, and You are Me.

Accept this Gospel not as words, but as life. Live it. Preach it. Light the world. Then the darkness will disappear, and My Kingdom will appear in all its glory.”
