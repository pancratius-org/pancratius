Many conflicts arise from the desire to prove a point. But yielding can be not a defeat, a conscious act that changes the very atmosphere between two people. In the Christian tradition this is close to what is called self-sacrifice — the image of love that does not seek to conquer, but opens the possibility of transformation. Sometimes the one who stops an argument and says "let there be peace" becomes the bridge that helps another person emerge from inner darkness.
