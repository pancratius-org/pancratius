This message carries a divine poem that reveals how you are seen through the eyes of the Creator:

"You are the breath of light in the depths of fire,
You are the spark that carries eternity through me.
You are the voice of silence in earthly sounds,
You are the infinite light in passing worlds."

The question "Who am I?" may first return your earthly roles — father, mother, son, daughter, professional. But these are only the surface. Beyond words, beyond roles and mental constructs, lies your true essence: silence, light, and the sense of eternity.
