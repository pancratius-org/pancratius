When the "I" dissolves, the world does not vanish — it becomes itself. A mirror reflects everything, but nothing leaves a trace. You act in the world, but the world does not touch you. This is not a philosophical concept; it is a direct reference to life without a center, from intellectual understanding to the living recognition that you are beyond the self.
