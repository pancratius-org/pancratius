The questions that sound within each of us — "Where are You when I cannot hear You?" "Why do You speak through simple things and not through miracles?" "What makes me capable of hearing You?" — receive here a frank, clear, and tender response.

This is not wordplay, and not a religious ritual. It is a meeting. Real. Deep. Alive. If you feel that this Voice touches you, don't turn away. This is a call. This is the Love that has always waited. This is the Word that has always been within you — and now it wants to be heard.
