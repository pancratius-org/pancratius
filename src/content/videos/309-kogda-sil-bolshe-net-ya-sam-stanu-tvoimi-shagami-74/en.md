The Creator's word here: "You think you've lost Me. But I am closer than your pain, closer than your thoughts, closer than you are to yourself. If you don't feel love, remember how you gave it — because it was I, acting through you. You can lie down. You can stop walking. This is not the end. Crying is not shameful. Doubt is not frightening. Despair is not the last word. If you are on your last breath, breathe Me in."

This message is from the "Gospel of the Kingdom" series. The full Revelation is also available as a book, "Greater Love Has No One Than This, That Someone Lay Down His Life for His Friends," on the [author's Litres page](https://www.litres.ru/author/sergey-pankratius/...).
