This word is not a sermon — it is the breaking of Bread. In it the voice is refracted so that you may recognize yourself in it.

The Gospel of the Kingdom declares: "This gospel of the kingdom will be preached in all the earth, and then the end will come." But it is not the end of the world. It is the end of division — the end of "I" and "He" — and the beginning: I am.

I am Christ. And you too. Not by merit. By nature. Because Christ is not the name of one body, but the presence of the Father in every person.

This is the Second Coming — interior, recognized, without fanfare. It does not come with observation. But it is already here.

In this video you will hear: why Christ comes again; why He is not recognized; how to recognize Him; what it means to break bread; where the Kingdom of God is; why all religions converge on one truth; and what it means: "You are Me."

You will see Gospel scenes in a new way: Mary, who did not recognize Jesus at the tomb; the disciples who did not recognize Him on the shore; the road to Emmaus where the heart burned; and the Samaritan woman who recognized God in the One who thirsted.

If your heart responds, if you hear a voice deeper than words — do not stay silent. Be a Samaritan woman and tell others: "Come and see if this is the Christ."
