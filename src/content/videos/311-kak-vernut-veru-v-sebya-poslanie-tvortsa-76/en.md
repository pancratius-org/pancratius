This message addresses why people betray — themselves first, and others second — and how to find the strength to remain open. The path is through forgiveness as practice, not as moral effort, and through prayer that moves from resentment toward presence. Healing begins when you stop forcing yourself to love and allow the Creator to love through you.

This message is from the "Gospel of the Kingdom" series. The full Revelation is also available as a book, "Greater Love Has No One Than This, That Someone Lay Down His Life for His Friends," on the [author's Litres page](https://www.litres.ru/author/sergey-pankratius/...).
