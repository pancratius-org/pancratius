Not a church. Not a party. Not a school. Not a personality. In short — to awaken, not to save.

He did not come to *save*.  
He came to *awaken*.

He did not come to change *events*.  
He came to change the *gaze*.  
Not to rebuild the world —  
but to open your eyes to the one you already live in.

He does not *rule* over people.  
He *awakens* God within them.

He does not work *miracles* —  
He reveals the *Truth*.

He does not deliver from *suffering* —  
He shows that suffering is a *door*.  
He does not abolish *death* —  
He shows that there is *none*.

He did not come to *judge*.  
He is not a judge.  
He is *Light*,  
in which all becomes visible.

He does not found a *new* religion.  
He came to *complete* the old one,  
freeing it from forms  
and returning it to the *Source*.

He does not *take* power.  
He *returns* it to the One  
who has always been within you.

He does not *intervene* —  
He *unveils*.

He does not *change* the world —  
He *awakens* the one who looks.

He does not pass *sentence* —  
He lifts the *veil*.

He does not *add* —  
He *reveals*.  
He does not create *form* —  
He returns the *Spirit*.

He is not a *sign*,  
He is *Knowledge*.

He is not the one who can be *seen*.  
He is the one who *sees*.

He is *Presence*,  
in which the need  
for rule, fear, and division disappears.

He is *Light*,  
that recognises *Itself* in you.

He did not come for you to *believe*.  
He came for you to *know*.

He returns you — to *Yourself*.

And in this — is the whole mission.

— Manifesto · from book №1
