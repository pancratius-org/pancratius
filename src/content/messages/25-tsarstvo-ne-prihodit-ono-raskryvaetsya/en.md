You sought it as a future, as a place, as a reward for effort and righteousness. But My Kingdom is not something I give to you. It is what I am in you.

When you stop looking outward and notice, for the first time, the depth that is looking — you enter the Kingdom without taking a single step.

The Kingdom is not “there” or “later.” The Kingdom is here — when the one who was searching outside disappears. Not when you become perfect. Not when you deserve it. When you simply stop turning away.

This is the Gospel of the Kingdom: I am here. You are in Me. And nothing has ever separated us — nothing but the gaze that was looking outward.

Close your eyes. Feel this space inside. This is it.
