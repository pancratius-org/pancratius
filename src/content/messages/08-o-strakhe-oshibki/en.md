You are afraid of making a mistake. But a mistake is not the end. A mistake is the path to Me.

I am not calling you to be flawless. I am calling you to be alive.

You are learning to walk, like a child — and your falls do not push you away from Me, they bring you closer. Every fall dissolves the illusion of self-sufficiency and opens the heart.

I do not look at your failures. I look at your heart. And if it is seeking Me — you are already on the Way. Not because you have walked without mistakes. But because you are walking.

Make mistakes — but do not lose the Thirst. Wander — but do not lose the Light. And if you fall — rise. Because I am near. And I do not step away while you are walking.

Perfection is a mask. A living heart — that is Mine.
