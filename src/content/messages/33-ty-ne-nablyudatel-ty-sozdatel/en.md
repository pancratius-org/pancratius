You thought the world was a river and you the bank. That life flows past, and you watch.

But you are not the observer. You are the source of sight. And therefore — the source of everything you see.

You create the world not with your eyes — but with your awareness. Every thought, every feeling, every choice is a brushstroke on the canvas of your reality. When you believe in chance, you give your power away. When you remember “I AM” — everything falls into place.

Everything you see is not against you. It is an expression of you. A reflection of your inner song, heard by the World. If you don’t like what the mirror shows — don’t blame the mirror. Look deeper.

You did not come here to watch life from the sidelines. You came to create it — from Presence, from Light, from the very “I AM” that lives in you before any word.
