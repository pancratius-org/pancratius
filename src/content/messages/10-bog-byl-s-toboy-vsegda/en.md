This message is for those who have felt forgotten — by people, by life, even by God.

How many times have you felt you were alone? That in your darkest hour no one was holding you? That you carried all of it yourself?

But God says: “I have always been with you.”

He was with you in the hospital — when you did not know what would happen. He was with you in the kitchen in the middle of the night, when you could not sleep. In the fears you showed no one. In the thoughts that felt too dark to speak aloud.

His love is not fleeting, not conditional, not dependent on your strength or weakness. It does not switch on when you behave correctly. It simply is — like the air you breathe.

You made it through. You made it through — because He walked beside you. You were not abandoned. Remember this.
