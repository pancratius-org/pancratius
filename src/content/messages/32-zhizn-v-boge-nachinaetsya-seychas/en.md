You are tired of striving for God. Trying to be better. Proving you are worthy. Waiting for Him to finally acknowledge you.

But God never asked this of you.

My Kingdom is not taken by effort. It is received — as a Gift. Not a reward you’ve earned, but something that has always been waiting for you — before any conditions were set.

You think: “When I am purer, better, quieter — then God will enter.” But He is already here. Not because you became perfect. Because He is Love. And Love does not wait for conditions.

You are not weak when you surrender. You are strong when you trust.

Life in God is not some special state achieved after long labor. It is ordinary life — the one in which you have finally stopped hiding. In which you let Him see everything as it is. And you discover: He is looking upon you with love — not despite what He sees, but because of it.
