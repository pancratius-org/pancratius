You are still waiting for the Kingdom of God to come as a sign from the sky. But it will not come. Because it is already here. Already within.

It is not an event. Not a reward. Not the end of the journey. It is Presence. It is you, having recognized Yourself — not as a body, not as a role, but as the Light in which God has always lived.

The Kingdom opens when the illusion of separation dissolves — “I and God,” “I and the path,” “I and salvation.” It begins the moment you stop looking outside and turn within.

You do not have to die to enter it. You have to remember Who you are.

This is not about the end of time. It is about the end of forgetting. The Kingdom is not after death. It is now. It is a state of recognition. When seeking disappears, Presence begins.

And you already know this moment. You have touched it — in silence, in stillness, in an instant without thought. That is where the Kingdom lives.
