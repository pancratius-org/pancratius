This is a call. If you hear it — it was meant for you.

How long have you been searching? Looking for a sign, for permission, for confirmation. Waiting for someone to say, “Go.” But everything you have been searching for is already here. Inside you.

Don’t wait for signs. Don’t wait for permission. The Light you are seeking outside — that is the Light you carry within. It has not gone out. It has simply been waiting for you to stop looking away.

You are the one you have been waiting for.

Wake. Not to rush somewhere new. But to stop, and see: here it is, right here. This is the time to remember who you are. This is the time to see the Light within. This is the time to move — not forward into noise, but inward — toward yourself.

The heart that remembers is already on the way.
