Stop. Feel this moment.

There is no past here, no future — only now. Everything you have searched for is already here. Everything you ever hoped to become, you already are. In this moment lives the Light of your true nature.

How often we tell ourselves: “I’ll be happy later, when I finish this. When everything falls into place.” But life doesn’t happen later — it happens now, this once.

Listen. Not to your thoughts about yourself, but to presence itself in this instant. Beneath all the anxieties and expectations, something stands quiet and steady. That is you.

Life is not a path toward unfolding. It is the unfolding itself. Eternity within you is not waiting for the right moment — it is already here, in you, now.
