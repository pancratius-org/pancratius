What is the Second Coming?

Not an event on the horizon. Not a figure in the clouds. Not a date on a calendar.

The Second Coming is an event within you.

It is the moment you stop waiting for God outside — and discover He has always been within. It is the moment when the veil of your self — your fear, your pride, your habit of seeing yourself as separate — grows thin, and through it begins to shine the One who said to Moses: “I AM.”

Christ comes on the clouds. Yes. But these are not the clouds of heaven. They are the clouds of your own consciousness: your doubts, your hopes, your fears, your expectations. Everything that was obscuring the Light.

When they part — not because you conquered them by force, but because you simply stopped clinging — He is already here.

He was always here. This is the Coming.
