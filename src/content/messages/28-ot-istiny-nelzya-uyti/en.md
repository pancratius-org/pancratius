You cannot leave the truth. Because truth is not a place and not a teaching. Truth is I Myself. And there is nothing outside Me, just as there is no place outside the light when the sun rises.

A person cannot depart from truth — they can only look away. Not leave, but not see. Not lose, but forget. Not cease to be in My Light, but close their eyes and call it dark.

As a molecule of water does not leave the ocean, even when it rises into a cloud — so the soul does not leave the truth, even when it wanders. She is always in Me.

Therefore do not seek your return as a long journey. Simply open your eyes. You have not gone far — you were simply looking the other way.

Truth does not accuse you for wandering. It simply waits — in this silence, in this moment, in this simple “I am” — until you remember that you never left.
