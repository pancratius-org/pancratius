You think God is far away. But God did not leave — you turned away.

He is closer than breath. Closer than thought. Closer than the tear — before it falls.

You search for Him in special places, in special words, in special people. But He is in you. In the most ordinary moment. When you wash your hands. When you look out the window. When you are silent — He is there.

You do not need to seek Him as a mystery. You need to recognize Him as Truth. The difference is vast: a mystery is sought from outside; Truth is known from within.

Don’t wait for a sign. Open your eyes. He is already here. And always has been.

In this ordinary breath — He is. In the silence behind words — He is. In this simple awareness of “I am” — He is.
