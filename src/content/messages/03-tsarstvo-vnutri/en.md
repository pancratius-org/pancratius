Are you searching for God ahead of you — in the next turn, the next revelation, the next prayer? Stop.

The Kingdom of God is not there. It is within you. Not as an idea, not as a future reward, but as a living reality — right now, in this breath.

How often we seek Him far away, building roads to reach Him — through discipline, through ritual, through merit. But He is not behind a door that still must be opened. He is on this side of every door. He is in the very space where you now stand.

Step inward — not forward, inward. Toward what is silent beneath your words. Toward what is present even while you think you’re searching. There lives the Light that never went anywhere. There lives the Love that never grows tired.

You are not walking toward God. You are remembering that you were always with Him.
