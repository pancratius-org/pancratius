Waiting is not hope. It is Presence postponed. It is the hidden belief: “Now is not enough. Now is without God. When things are different — then everything will change.”

But God has not left you for a single moment.

Every “someday” moves you away from Him. Every “later” blinds you to what is already shining inside. You do not need the future to be with God. You need recognition. Now.

In waiting, God becomes a goal — out there, ahead, past conditions. In Presence, God becomes you — here, in this breath.

Awakening does not come later. It happens when you stop waiting. When you realize: everything you were waiting for — is you. In Light. In Truth. In God.

Stop. Not later — now. See what is already here.
