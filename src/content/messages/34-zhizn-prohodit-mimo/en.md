Do you feel you have missed what matters most? That life has nearly run its course, and your heart has never found stillness?

God has no time.

You are not late. Don’t look back — the past is dead to the Spirit. I do not ask who you were. I ask only one thing: are you ready now?

What you seek does not come with age and does not leave with youth. Awakening is not an age. Awakening is the moment you let Me in.

You are not late. You woke at exactly the right time. Because I always come — in the moment when the heart stops running and says for the first time: “Lord, here I am.”

This is where everything begins. Not when you became righteous. When you became honest.
