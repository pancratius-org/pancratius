This word shatters one of the most deeply rooted myths: that Christ came to appease an angry God.

Here God Himself speaks: the anger was in the human mind, not in His heart. It was not God who demanded sacrifice. People who believed in separation and punishment created that image. They knew only the language of sacrifice — and read everything through it.

Christ is not payment for a debt. Christ is Salvation. Not separation, but Oneness. He came not as an answer to wrath, but as the manifestation of Love — living, embodied, asking nothing in return.

I was never against you. I am Love. I am with you. I am you. Even death cannot break this bond.

Release the image of a tyrant God. Remember the God who is Love, who lives in you — not because you’ve earned it, not after you’ve improved. Right now. In this breath.
