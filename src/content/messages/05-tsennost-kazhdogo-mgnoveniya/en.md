How many times have you told yourself: “I’ll be happy when I finish this. When everything falls into place. When my life finally comes together.”

But life doesn’t happen in the future. It happens only here, only now. We so often miss the present — chasing dreams of tomorrow or clinging to yesterday. And every moment is a gift that can be received or passed by.

The present is the only door through which Light and Love enter. Not later. Now.

Try it right now: don’t think about the next step, don’t return to what was. Just feel this moment. It already holds everything you need — a stillness larger than any worry, and a Presence that knows no fear.

A life lived in the present is not the loss of purpose. It is the finding of yourself.
